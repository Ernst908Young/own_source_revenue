Start a New Business

  • About
  • Start a New Business

The Single Window Clearance System will assist you in obtaining the requisite clearances/approvals to establish and operate a business unit and to apply for In-principle approvals.


Preparation of Detailed Project Report

The Entrepreneur has to prepare a technically feasible and economically viable Project Report for the line of activity chosen. The Entrepreneur may seek assistance from various State Govt. & Central Govt. agencies like Department of Industries, DIPP, etc. They may also seek assistance from various CA, CS, independent consultants, consultancy firms and industry experts.


Finance

The Entrepreneur has to plan for mobilisation of funds as per the cost of the project both including Fixed Capital Cost & Working Capital for business operation. The Entrepreneur may preferably obtain the consent of a financing agency such as bank and financial institutions for the required loan amount.


In Principle Approvals

The entrepreneur has to register with their details in Single Window Portal for creation of Unique User ID & password. They need to provide the basic information on the proposed business establishment through filling of Common Application Form as prescribed by the Department of Industries.
This “In-Principle Approval” only means, the concerned authority has agreed to the proposal without getting into the details of other required statutory/legal compliances. This enables the beneficiary to obtain the approvals/sanctions at a comfortable pace and ease. However by no means In-principle approval can be considered as final approval and so they are subject to proper verification as per the rules of law.


Commencement of Operations

After obtaining all the approvals from the competent authorities, the Entrepreneur has to start civil construction & place orders for the machinery and after completing civil construction & erection of the Machinery; the Entrepreneur has to commence Commercial Production within the time period stipulated by different Govt. agencies and Departments. Hence after, the MSMEs have to obtain acknowledgement of UAM from the competent authority and all the large Industries has to obtain IEM Part -B from Department of Industrial Policy & Promotion, Ministry of Commerce & Industries.
